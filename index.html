Goa Trip Itinerary

Saturday (Arrival & Explore North Goa)

  • Check-in at Para
  • Lunch: Vinayak Family Restaurant / Mojigao Cafe
  • Visit Chapora Fort & Vagator Viewpoint
  • Dinner: Artjuna or Soro Village Pub

Sunday (Panjim & Fontainhas)

  • Brunch at Baba au Rhum
  • Explore Fontainhas, Altinho Viewpoint
  • Dona Paula Viewpoint / Mandovi River Cruise (optional)
  • Dinner at The Black Sheep Bistro or Mum’s Kitchen

Monday (Check-out & Move to South Goa)

  • Check-out → Scenic drive to South Goa
  • Stop at Three Kings Chapel en route
  • Check-in at Hotel Silva Heritage
  • Lunch at Martin’s Corner

Tuesday (Explore South Goa)

  • Visit Rachol Seminary Church / Chandor Heritage Homes
  • Lunch at Fisherman’s Wharf or Mike’s Place
  • Optional: Scenic drive to Palolem or Agonda viewpoints
